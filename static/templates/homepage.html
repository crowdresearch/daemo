ABOUT US
Daemo was created via a world-wide, crowdsourced research process initi-
ated at Stanford University. We are a multidisciplinary group of researchers
who gathered together with the only purpose of creating Daemo as crowd-
sourcing platform that instills trust and fairness among its members.
